# Feature Ideas for Quizzr

## Recently shipped
- ✅ Co-owned note sets with collaborator invites and shared flashcards.
- ✅ Public quiz template gallery plus classroom challenge links.
- ✅ AI-generated flashcards and dedicated study view.

## Study Experience
- **Adaptive spaced repetition playlists**: Resurface missed questions automatically over days/weeks with adjustable reminder cadence.
- **Confidence calibration mode**: Ask learners to rate confidence before answering, then show calibration charts to improve metacognition.
- **Audio questions and answers**: Let users record themselves explaining concepts and turn transcripts into quiz prompts.

## Collaboration & Sharing
- **Co-owned note sets**: Invite classmates to contribute to the same note set with role-based permissions and change history.
- **Public template gallery**: Curate the best community-created quizzes/notes for reuse, with tagging and search filters.
- **Classroom challenges**: Generate shareable challenge codes so small groups can race through the same quiz and compare leaderboards.

## Analytics & Guidance
- **Personalized study roadmap**: Aggregate attempt data to highlight weak topics, recommended readings, and optimal next quizzes.
- **Goal tracking widgets**: Let students set weekly quiz/score targets and visualize streaks directly on the dashboard.
- **Curriculum alignment**: Map questions to standards (AP, SAT, etc.) so learners can track mastery by syllabus objective.

## Platform Integrations
- **Learning management system sync**: Pull assignments or reading lists from Canvas/Google Classroom to auto-create note sets.
- **Calendar reminders**: Push retake reminders to Google/Apple Calendar with deep links back into the quiz attempt.
- **Cloud storage imports**: Support ingesting PDFs/Docs from Drive, Dropbox, or Notion pages for richer note sources.
